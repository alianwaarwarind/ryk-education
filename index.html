The Cost of Not Learning: Rahim Yar Khan

In the district of Rahim Yar Khan, a staggering $\mathbf{673,000}$ children between the ages of 5 and 16 are currently out of school.

But a statistic is not just a number. It is a child. It is a dream.

Meet Ali. He represents just one of these children. He dreams of becoming a pilot, soaring above the very fields where he now toils, never having stepped inside a classroom.

But Ali is not alone.
